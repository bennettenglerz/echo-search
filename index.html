ECHO

A revolutionary housing search tool designed to improve the housing search process for low-income families in Greater Boston and around the country.

  • Get Started

Our Purpose

At ECHO, we believe the housing search process doesn’t have to be a headache. That’s why we’ve designed a digital interface that brings together information on neighborhoods, public transportation, and housing unit availability to make the housing search process as easy as possible for low-income families looking to find housing in the neighborhood that’s right for them.

Our Work

We’ve designed phase one of the website and are currently piloting it with shelters across the state trying to help homeless families find shelter. In the next phase, we’ll be moving to mobile, adding in a number of helpful features that will enable families to learn more about potential neighborhoods and search through a variety of housing search websites, and rolling out the tool more widely.

Who We Are

ECHO is designed by researchers at MIT, the Boston Housing Authority and partners throughout the Greater Boston area.
